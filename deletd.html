Account Deletion

🗑️

Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your Star Kids account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed.
📱

Method 1: Delete from App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account:

  1. Open the Star Kids app
  2. Tap the Menu icon (top right)
  3. Go to My Profile
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion in the dialog

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

📧

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send us an email:

Email: developer3716.design@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - Star Kids

Include:

  • Your registered email or username
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 7 business days.

📋

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account (name, email, username, password)
  • All children profiles you created
  • All activity records (good/bad deeds)
  • All app preferences and settings
⏱️

Deletion Timeline

In-app deletion: Immediate

Email request: Within 7 business days

After deletion, you will be logged out automatically and cannot recover your data.
